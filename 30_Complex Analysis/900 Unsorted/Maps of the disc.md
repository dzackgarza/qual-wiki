:::{.theorem title="Biholomorphisms of the disc"}
\[
\Aut_\CC(\DD) = \ts{ z\mapsto e^{i\theta} \qty{\alpha - z \over 1 - \bar{\alpha} z} }
.\]
:::

:::{.proof title="?"}
Schwarz lemma.
:::


:::{.theorem title="?"}
\[
\Aut_\CC(\HH) = \ts{ z \mapsto {az+b \over cz+d} \st a,b,c,d\in \CC, ad-bc=1 } \cong\PSL_2(\RR)
.\]
:::
